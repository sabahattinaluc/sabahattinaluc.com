About

I'm a designer and strategist based in Košice, Slovakia, working with clients across Europe and beyond. I've spent fifteen years helping startups, cultural institutions, and established brands figure out who they are — and then building the visual and digital presence to match.

Bright Leaf Design is my practice. The name comes from a moment in a Prague winter: every other tree was bare, but one oak on a hillside was still holding its leaves — warm, golden, defiant. I try to bring that quality to the work: things that hold their character when everything else is falling into noise.

Before going independent, I led design at two agencies in Vienna and Bratislava, where I learned that the best design work comes from genuine curiosity about a client's world — not just the brief they hand you.
